The BackStreeT

ABOUT

The BackStreet were born in 1985. They grew up in the 18th arrondissement of London, where they were graffitis artists. Thay made their debut in rap in 2000 with the slogan "independence". Author-composer-performer, they take care of the entire musical production chain: recording, mixing, webmastering and computer graphics. They perform both in groups, with the 306 Crew, or in solo with PeeJay. They first became known under the pseudonym 306 Crew, before becoming The BackStreeT. A figure in the French underground rap, they cultivate a discreet, even mysterious attitude. The simplicity of the character they embodie arouses enthusiasm and a strong identification. Refusing media coverage, they filled concert halls without advertising, interviews or guest on their albums...
